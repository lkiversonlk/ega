Welcome to EEGalaxy

the game is in test, please make sure your metamask are connecting to Ropsten testnet!

EEGalaxy is a virtual world game based on ethereum, this planet is divided into many grids that player could buy, sell and upgrade it, and even build your own fleet。
Every land owner can share the revenue of this world.


Ex: if there are 4 lands sold out to A, B and C (A 2, B 1, C 1)，when A bought another ownerless land in price of 1ETH, 0.5ETH goes to the galaxy, the other 0.5ETH will be distributed to B, C equally.
or D bought one of A's land in 3ETH, 2.7ETH goes to A，0.15ETH goes to galaxy, the 0.15ETH left will be distributed to A, B and C equally (since A still have one lands)

in one world, all the galaxy gain will be shared by land owners according to the count of their lands

Operation

after loading the game, the status panel is in the left side, which consist of world status and player status.

Grids count the total number of the grids

Sold the number of grids sold out, one grid is calculated only once

Fee the charge rate of transaction in the world

Minimal price the minimum price of a unsold grid

there are two switches control the display of grids network and the project grid of the mouse.

player status shows player's logo, address and how much he earns, how many lands he owns.

click your logo to upload new logo, this requires a sign to verify your identity, it doesn't cost ETH.

click Claim to get your earnings in the world.

Operation panel on the right side.
On top is the status of grid that mouse poining

grid mark the No. of grid you're pointing.

lon lat longitude, latitude of your mouse

logo the logo of the owner of the grid you're pointing.

you may click on the planet to choose land.
or enter grid No. in the search input and click ok.

Search display the Grid No. you selected.
you can use it to select grid, too.

Grid
once grid is selected, it will display the picture set by the grid owner
if you own the land，click the picture to load new pic.
you can click sell to set a price for the land.
if you do not own the land and if it's on sell, you may buy this land.

Notice uploading logo, change grid pic doesn't cost your ETH, it only needs a sign operation to verify you're the owner.

Trading

the whole planet is devided into lands, every land is ownerless .
you can buy it at minimal price.
if a land is bought by some player, it becomes owned , it can't be bought, player can decorate their lands
you can also set a price to sell your lands.

when a ownerless land is sold out, the money will be distributed to the galaxy and current land owners of the world. (exclude the buyer)。

when one player's land is bought by others, the money except the fee will go to the seller's account.
the fee will be distributed to the galaxy and current land owners of the world. (exclude the buyer)。

Claim the money player earns is in the contract, you may find the value in the earns.

click Claim to transfer the ETH to your account.

Contract

Contract is displayed in the dropdown list of the network button in the navbar.
